    Welcome to Coffee Addict House


     We hope you enjoy our espresso drinks, real fruit and local honey smoothies, hot breakfast and      lunch panini sandwiches, cold deli sandwiches, wraps and salads, bulk coffee beans, gifts, used      books and CDs, and free Wi-Fi. We have pleasant outlook seating including a front deck with a large      shade tree growing through it and a secret garden with fish pond out back.

     We committed to offer world-class but affordable products and first-rate service. Our enthusiasm      and respect for culture, nature, people and quality are the heart of Coffee Addict. We recognize and      harnessed the opportunity to create great change in the coffee industry by reserving and roasting      some of our own beans. We had all the ingredients for success, access to the quality gourmet coffee      beans from the county's best plantation, Benguet-grown Arabica coffee, Arabica coffee is best      grown in the highlands, coffee experts say, and this is proven again winning three spots in the top      six of the 2018 Philippines Coffee Quality Competition (PCQC)
